My Story

Hello there! I'm Cristian Gutierrez, a senior at the University of Central Florida majoring in Computer Engineering. As a first-generation engineering student hailing from a Colombian family, I've always been driven to excel and make my mark in the world of technology. Growing up in Florida, I developed a passion for tinkering with electronics, designing web and mobile applications, and exploring the exciting realms of embedded software and front-end development. Beyond my academic pursuits, I have a range of interests that keep me engaged and inspired. Whether it's kicking a soccer ball on the field, immersing myself in captivating video games, or experimenting with new culinary creations in the kitchen, I always find joy in what I do. My enthusiastic and creative nature, coupled with my diligent work ethic, enables me to thrive both individually and in group settings. With a solid foundation in computer engineering and a strong desire to learn and grow, I am eager to embark on new challenges and contribute my skills to cutting-edge projects. I'm ready to seize opportunities in embedded software or front-end roles, where I can combine my technical expertise with my innate passion for innovation.

StudyFlow

 

StudyFlow is a streamlined, Python-based productivity tool for learners, especially in programming. It automates the setup of an optimal study environment, including launching a coding environment, note-taking app, and a focus-enhancing Lo-Fi music stream. Its core feature, a responsive Pomodoro timer, promotes efficient time management. With a user-friendly GUI and customizability, StudyFlow offers an efficient, tailored study experience.

Check it out!

Fantasy Assistant

     

Fantasy Assistant is a Chrome extension crafted for soccer fans, particularly those engaged in Fantasy Premier League soccer. This tool utilizes JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to provide a user-friendly experience, incorporating features such as player search and data presentation through asynchronous API integration and performance optimization.
